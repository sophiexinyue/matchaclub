Our Club

Welcome to Columbia Matcha Club! We are the official club for the Columbia and Barnard community of matcha lovers!

We've built this website as a hub for all things matcha, including recipes, reviews, and our favorite brands. We also have a Slack channel where we share matcha-related news and events.

About Us

The Columbia/Barnard Matcha Club is a community of students who share a love for matcha and its many forms. Co-founded and led by Co-Presidents Anna Hammell and Sophie Pan, the club brings together students to explore, taste, and learn about matcha.

We host regular taste-testing events, share our findings on different brands and brewing methods, and organize outings to local NYC matcha spots. Beyond the fun, we take pride in educating our members about the historical and cultural significance of matcha, particularly its roots in Japanese tea ceremonies. We also explore its wellness benefits—like antioxidants and naturally calming caffeine—as a mindful alternative to other energizers.

Through these experiences, we aim to foster a welcoming, relaxed, and informative space where students can slow down, sip intentionally, and celebrate matcha together.
